Something about me

I am a pasonate amature maker of things and Software Engineer student! ...

I usually take projects way too big for my skill and try to make'em work. Problem solving is a part of everything I do! New challanges are a good way to push my skill forward and thats why I try to work in a lot of diferent fields. Always loved to craft and make, but it all really started when I wanted to make my D&D game more fun. Started with simple cardboard tiles and black and white acylic colors. Building up my material and color stash until I bought a 3D printer. It all sky rocketed from there. Now my room is not a room anymore but a workshop with a bed!

I make custom things and if you are interested in something or have an idea! You can contact me and we will see if I can make something for you!
